How to use SkillMapper

Turn a plain English description of what you need into the exact job title, salary range, and candidate profile — in seconds.

1

Three ways to find any role

SkillMapper has 188 roles across 18 departments. Pick the entry point that matches what you know:

Add skills — you know the skills needed but not the exact title.

Browse by title — you know the job title. Search or scroll the full role list.

Describe what you need — you know the problem but not the role.
2

Read the result cards

Each card shows the required skills, nice-to-have skills, salary by level in INR and USD, and an amber hiring tip.

💰 Salary data: Glassdoor India, AmbitionBox, Naukri — Q4 2025 benchmarks. INR = CTC (base + PF + variable). Verify at ambitionbox.com before making an offer.
3

Read what SkillMapper tells you

Every result card shows: what the role actually does, the skills required, market salary by level, and a smart hiring tip specific to that role.

4

Source the candidate in TalentXRay

Once you have the right role, click "Search in TalentXRay". It opens pre-filled with the job title and top skills.

Pro tips
🎯

Be specific about the problem

Don't say "marketing person" — say "grow our user base organically through content and SEO".

🔢

Match % is relative to your input

60% with 2 skills entered is a strong signal. 20% with 8 skills entered is weak.

⚠️

Read the amber tip on every card

These tips are written from real JD patterns.

💼

Check alternative titles

The "Also called" section shows all different names for the same role.

💰

Salaries vary by city

Bangalore, Mumbai, and Delhi pay 20–40% above the ranges shown.

📊

Use the dept filter for broad searches

Use the Data department filter to see only data roles and compare side by side.
